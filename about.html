About Autonomix

Pioneering AI automation and digital innovation from Benghazi, Libya.

Our Story

Founded by Osama Ellafi and Zakaria Eltawill, Autonomix is the first-of-its-kind company in Libya specializing in AI automation services, including intelligent workflows and AI agents, alongside expert web and mobile app development. Based in Benghazi, we bridge cutting-edge global technology with local innovation to empower businesses in the region and beyond.

Our mission is to redefine automation and digital transformation, delivering premium, futuristic solutions that drive real results.
